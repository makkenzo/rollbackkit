# Code of Conduct

RollbackKit contributors are expected to keep technical discussion direct, specific and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Focus criticism on code, architecture, behavior and tradeoffs.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence.
- Keep disagreements actionable.
- Respect maintainers' decisions about scope and timing.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests after a maintainer decision.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block accounts when
behavior makes the project unsafe or unproductive for contributors.
